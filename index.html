About Me

My name is Cebrail, and I hold a Master's degree in Applied Mathematics and Data Science from the University of Lyon 1.

I began my career as a Mathematics teacher, where I spent nearly a year teaching Mathematics to students. I then joined the research team at IFSTTAR, developing a machine learning model to predict road traffic injuries in France using data from law enforcement and hospitals.

After this, I took on the role of Data Engineer - Data Scientist at EVS Professionnelle, where I have been involved in a range of data-driven tasks. My responsibilities include collaborating with Data Analysts to build databases, developing web interfaces for APIs, and creating predictive models.

Passionate about what I do, I’m excited to take the next step in my career as a freelancer. I look forward to embracing new challenges and leveraging my expertise to help businesses optimize their data strategies and drive growth.
